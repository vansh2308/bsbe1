Exercise
Protein Structure Alignment
Retrieve 3D structural data of two proteins from the PDB database (1ORR and 1I2B).
Try to look for these information from the PDB file:
  • What proteins are they?
  • By what method they are crystalized? How many chains/ subunits do they contain?
  • What is their oligomeric/quaternary state?
Visualize in Pymol/MOL*:
  • Visualize the protein in molecular surface representation.
  • Do they look similar? If so, how similar?
Compare the structure using MMalign:
  • What is their RMSD?
  • What is their TM-score?
  • Can you say are they similar or not?
